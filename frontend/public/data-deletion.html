Data Deletion Request

Last updated: March 20, 2026

You have the right to request deletion of all personal data we hold about you. This includes your account information, connected platform tokens, usage history, and any content stored on our servers.

What gets deleted

  • Your account (name, email, password hash)
  • Connected platform OAuth tokens (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook)
  • AI usage history and generated content
  • Scheduled posts and campaign data
  • Uploaded videos and thumbnails
  • Subscription and billing records (except those required by law)

How to request deletion

Option 1 — Email us

Send an email from your registered account email address to request deletion. We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation.

Response time

We will confirm receipt of your request within 5 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days. You will receive an email confirmation once your data has been deleted.
